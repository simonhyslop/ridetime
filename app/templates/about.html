How does it work?

Tell us where you want to start your ride and we'll create a circular route ending back at the same place.

With RideTime, all routes are circular - so they start and finish in the same location.

Perfect for planning rides with friends, riding solo, weekend rides or after-work exercise.

It's totally free to use. Ready to get started?


Do I need an account?

No, you can get started with route planning straight away, no need to sign up.

If you want to though, you can use an account to save routes and come back and view them again later.
